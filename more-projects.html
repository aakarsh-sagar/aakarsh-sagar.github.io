Football Player Tracking

In this Computer Vision Project, my aim is to utilize the YOLOv5 model trained on a custom dataset to detect players and other relevant entities in the video.
The detected objects are subsequently tracked across frames using the ByteTrack algorithm.
To enhance the visual presentation, I replaced traditional bounding boxes with more visually appealing annotations such as ellipses.
My approach is evaluated on the "DFL Bundesliga Data Shootout" dataset from Kaggle and a custom dataset from Roboflow.
